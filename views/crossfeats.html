Men's Trivia:

For four years, from 2011 to 2014, Rich Froning, Jr. has held the title of "Fittest Man on Earth" at the CrossFit Games. He decided to go with a team this year and ended up winning with CrossFit Mayhem Freedom.



Women's Trivia:

Every year, since 2007, there has been a different CrossFit Games Champion in the Women's competition except for Icelandic Annie Thorisdottir who was the "Fittest Woman on Earth" in 2011 and 2012.

Union Strong






I’ve been doing CrossFit for almost three years. I started out in January 2013 with a local affiliate in the Philippines called CrossFit Insurrecto and I’ve been hooked ever since.

Upon joining CrossFit Union Square last October 2014, despite suffering wrist injuries in November 2014 due to a bad lift, I found myself besting my personal records, breaking through my plateau and getting the Holy Grail of CrossFit movements – the muscle up.

With well-planned and intense programming at CrossFit Union Square, all of my one-repetition maxes in the Philippines then are now my 3 to 10 rep maxes varying in reps based on the lifts. I’m back and I’m better.
